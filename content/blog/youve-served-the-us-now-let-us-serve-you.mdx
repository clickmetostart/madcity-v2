<p>Veteran’s Administration or “VA” loans are available for active, non-active, and retired Army, Air Force, Marine, Navy, National Guard, and Coast Guard vets who meet the established service requirements.</p>



<p>The most notable features and benefits for those who qualify are:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>100% financing/No down payment</li>



<li>No monthly mortgage insurance (PMI)</li>



<li>Gift funds acceptable for closing costs</li>



<li>No cash reserve requirements</li>



<li>A variety of terms or loan types available</li>



<li>Available for purchase and refinance</li>



<li>Reduced costs for disabled veterans</li>



<li>Seller can pay for closing costs</li>



<li>Seller pays for any required repairs</li>



<li>No pre-payment penalty</li>
</ul>



<p>To determine your ability to participate in this program, just provide your Certificate of Eligibility (COE) or your Discharge/Separation form (DD214). If you do not have your COE, you can request one using form 26-1880.</p>



<p>Reach out to one our ProVisors Rob Miller, and he’ll be happy to explain the details and answer any questions you may have.</p>
